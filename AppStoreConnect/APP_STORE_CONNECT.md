## English Localization

Promotional text:
A fast, minimal stickman battle royale you can play completely offline.

Description:
Lastman is a top-down stickman battle royale built for quick solo matches.

Enter a dark arena, move with the left joystick, aim with the right joystick, hide in bushes, and survive as the safe zone closes in. Bots hunt, dodge, shoot, and fight to stay alive too.

Features:
- Solo battle royale against bots
- Fully offline gameplay
- Simple, responsive twin-stick controls
- Bushes for hiding and ambushes
- Shrinking safe zone during each match
- Adjustable bot count and difficulty
- Clean black-and-white style with readable color accents

No account, no connection, no ads: just an arena, bots, and one clear goal. Be the last one standing.

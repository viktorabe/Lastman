A 90-second battle royale. Play the same daily challenge as your friends and beat their score.

Description:
Lastman is a top-down stickman battle royale built for quick, replayable matches.

Enter a dark arena, move with one thumb using auto-aim or use the right stick for precision. Hide in bushes and survive as the safe zone closes in.

Every day, everyone gets the same challenge: the same weapon, conditions, and goal. Set the best score, level up, complete missions, and challenge your friends.

- Solo battle royale against bots

- Levels, daily streak, missions, and weapon mastery
- Shareable scores and result cards
- Sound, impact, and haptic feedback

No account, no connection, no ads: just an arena, bots, and one clear goal. Be the last one standing.

The app has no proprietary account system, ads, in-app purchases, or external content. New players start with a guided match.
